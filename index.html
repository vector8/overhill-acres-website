About the Farm

Overhill Acres Farm is a small family farm tucked into the rolling countryside of Carleton, Nova Scotia. We've been working this land since 2022, raising bees, keeping a happy flock of laying hens, and growing more vegetables than we know what to do with each summer.

Everything we sell comes straight from our property — no middle steps, no long drives, no mystery. If you'd like to know exactly where your food comes from, you can come see for yourself.

What We Sell

Honey & Bee Products

Raw honey from our hives, plus seasonal bee products as they're available.

Farm-Fresh Eggs

Eggs from our pasture-raised hens, gathered daily.

Seasonal Vegetables

Whatever the garden gives us a surplus of — ask what's in season.

Where to Find Us

You can buy directly from the farm at 4749 Highway 340, Carleton, NS, or catch us at local farmers' markets throughout the season. Reach out if you're not sure when we'll be open — we're happy to set up a time.
